DEUX CORNICHONS BREWING COLLABORATIVE

Deux Cornichons first began when two of my closest friends were getting married (in which one is quickly becoming a master brewer) created wedding beers for favors. They called themselves Deux Cornichons. It then quickly became a collaborative when myself and three other friends joined. With the love of beer, art, friends, woodworking, and the internets, we became a brewing collaborative, brewing our own beer, creating custom artwork, building taps and kegerators and spreading a little love.

ADVANSTAR/IVT NETWORK

While working at Advanstar I had an assignment to create a cover for an product e-book. IVT network provides articles, videos, infographics, compendiums and quizzes for several pharma/biotech topics. It would become a toolkit that contained everything you need to know about a certain topic. Working with the marketing department we came up with a literal toolkit concept and within the box I illustrated the "tools" you would find within the book.
